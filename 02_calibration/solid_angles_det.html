Способ №3: по расчитанным угловым разрешениям матриц¶

($\rho_{\theta}$) ($\rho_{\varphi}$) : $$\Omega_{pix} (\theta)= \int_{\varphi}^{\varphi+\Delta\varphi_{pix}}\int_{\theta}^{\theta+\Delta\theta_{pix}} \sin\theta'd\theta'd\varphi'= 2\Delta \varphi_{pix}\sin(\theta+\frac{\Delta\theta_{pix}}{2})sin\frac{\Delta\theta_{pix}}{2}$$ , $\Delta\theta_{pix}=\frac{1}{\rho_{\theta}(\theta)}$, $\Delta\varphi_{pix}=\frac{1}{\rho_{\varphi}(\theta)}$, : $$ \Omega_{pix} (\theta)= \frac{2}{\rho_{\varphi}(\theta)}\sin(\theta+\frac{1}{2\rho_{\theta}(\theta)})\sin\frac{1}{2\rho_{\theta}(\theta)}$$
